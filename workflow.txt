================================================================================
SCHOLARHUB HPU – COMPLETE WORKFLOW GUIDE FOR STUDENTS
(Understandable to a layman / first-time user)
================================================================================

This document explains, step-by-step, what a student does on the ScholarHub HPU
system, how the HOD verifies the student, and what happens at each stage.

ScholarHub has two main portals:
  • ScholarSync  – for Ph.D. scholars (research thesis, supervisor, milestones)
  • ScholarTrack – for attendance, leave, and correction requests for all students

================================================================================
PART 1 – OPENING THE PORTAL AND SIGNING UP
================================================================================

1. GO TO THE WEBSITE
   - Open your web browser (Chrome / Edge / Firefox, etc.).
   - Type the portal address given by your department:
       ScholarSync:  https://sync.scholarhubhpu.in
       ScholarTrack: https://track.scholarhubhpu.in
   - Press Enter. The login page will open.

2. CHOOSE “SIGN UP”
   - On the login page, click the button or link that says “Sign Up” or
     “Register New Account”.

3. SELECT YOUR ROLE
   - If you are a research scholar (Ph.D. student), choose “Student”.
   - Faculty, HOD, and Admin accounts are normally created by the Super Admin,
     but in some cases a faculty member can also sign up on the portal.

4. FILL THE SIGN-UP FORM
   You will see a form. Fill it carefully:

   a. Full Name
      - Type your complete name as it appears in your university records.

   b. Email Address (Username)
      - This is your university email, e.g., scholar@hpu.ac.in.
      - This email will be used as your username every time you log in.

   c. Password
      - Create a strong password.
      - It must be at least 8 characters long.
      - It must contain at least:
          • one uppercase letter (A-Z)
          • one lowercase letter (a-z)
          • one digit (0-9)
          • one special symbol (!@#$%^&* etc.)
      - Keep it safe. Do not share it with anyone.

   d. Confirm Password
      - Re-type the same password to make sure there is no typing mistake.

   e. Department
      - Select your department from the dropdown list.

   f. Phone Number
      - Enter your 10-digit Indian mobile number.
      - The system automatically accepts numbers starting with 6, 7, 8, or 9.

   g. Degree / Academic Details (for students)
      - Academic Session: e.g., 2024-2025
      - Degree Type: e.g., Ph.D., M.Phil., M.A., M.Sc., etc.
      - Degree Name: e.g., Ph.D. in English, M.Sc. Physics, etc.
      - Semester / Year: if asked
      - Gender and Category: select from the dropdowns

5. SUBMIT THE FORM
   - Click the “Register” or “Sign Up” button.
   - The system will check:
       • Is the email already registered?
       • Is the phone number already registered?
       • Is the password strong enough?
   - If everything is correct, your account is created.

================================================================================
PART 2 – EMAIL VERIFICATION
================================================================================

6. CHECK YOUR EMAIL INBOX
   - After successful registration, the system sends a verification email to the
     email address you entered.
   - Open your email inbox (and also check the Spam / Junk folder).

7. CLICK THE VERIFICATION LINK
   - The email has a button “Verify Email Address”.
   - Click that button. It will open a new page in the browser.
   - If the button does not work, copy the long link below the button and paste
     it in the browser address bar.

8. VERIFICATION SUCCESSFUL
   - The page will show a success message: “Email verified successfully!”
   - Your email is now verified. You can log in.
   - Important: The verification link is valid only for 24 hours.

9. DID NOT RECEIVE THE EMAIL?
   - Go back to the login page and click “Resend Verification Email”.
   - Enter your email address and click “Resend”.
   - A new email will be sent.

================================================================================
PART 3 – LOGGING IN
================================================================================

10. RETURN TO THE LOGIN PAGE
    - Go to https://sync.scholarhubhpu.in or https://track.scholarhubhpu.in again.

11. ENTER YOUR CREDENTIALS
    - Username: your email address
    - Password: the password you created during signup

12. SELECT THE CORRECT PORTAL (if asked)
    - If you are a Ph.D. scholar, you must first log in to ScholarSync.
    - If you are a regular student (non-Ph.D.), you must use ScholarTrack.
    - The system will not allow:
        • Non-Ph.D. students to enter ScholarSync
        • Ph.D. students whose profile is not yet verified by HOD to enter
          ScholarTrack

13. CLICK “LOGIN”
    - If your email is verified and your account is active, the system will
      open your Dashboard.
    - If your email is not verified, you will see an error message and you must
      verify your email first.
    - If your account is disabled, you must contact your HOD.

================================================================================
PART 4 – STUDENT DASHBOARD AND PROFILE
================================================================================

14. WELCOME TO YOUR DASHBOARD
    - After login, you see the Dashboard.
    - The Dashboard shows useful information, for example:
        • Your name, roll number, department
        • Your verification status
        • Notifications (alerts/messages from HOD, faculty, or system)
        • Quick links to Profile, Thesis, Attendance, Leaves, etc.

15. COMPLETE YOUR PROFILE
    - Click on “Profile” or “Complete Profile” in the menu.
    - Fill all the required details:
        • Personal Details: date of birth, gender, category, nationality,
          father’s name, mother’s name, address
        • Contact Details: email, phone number
        • Academic Details: enrollment number, admission date, Ph.D. mode
          (full-time / part-time), academic session, degree, subject
        • Thesis Details: thesis title, thesis summary, keywords, preferred
          supervisor/guide
        • Qualifications: 10th, 12th, graduation, post-graduation, M.Phil, NET/JRF,
          fellowships, other certificates
        • For faculty: designation, specialization, office room, experience,
          publications, awards, etc.

16. UPLOAD YOUR PHOTO (AVATAR)
    - Click on your profile picture area.
    - Choose a recent passport-size photo from your computer or phone.
    - The system will save it as your profile picture.

17. UPLOAD DOCUMENTS / CERTIFICATES
    - In the profile section, you may see options to upload documents.
    - Click “Upload Certificate” next to the relevant qualification.
    - Select a PDF or image file (class 10, class 12, graduation, etc.).
    - Make sure the file is clear and readable.

18. REVIEW EVERYTHING
    - Before you submit, read every field carefully.
    - Wrong or incomplete information can cause your profile to be rejected by
      the HOD.

19. SUBMIT PROFILE FOR VERIFICATION
    - At the bottom of the profile page, click the button “Submit Profile” or
      “Complete Profile”.
    - The system will mark your profile as “Completed” and send it to the HOD
      for verification.
    - You will see a message: “Profile submitted successfully. Waiting for HOD
      verification.”

================================================================================
PART 5 – WHAT HAPPENS AT THE HOD END
================================================================================

20. HOD RECEIVES THE VERIFICATION REQUEST
    - The HOD (Head of Department) logs in to their HOD account.
    - In the HOD Dashboard, there is a section called “Department Users” or
      “Pending Verifications”.
    - The HOD sees your name in the list of students who have submitted their
      profiles.

21. HOD OPENS YOUR PROFILE
    - The HOD clicks on your name.
    - The system shows your complete profile and all uploaded documents.

22. HOD REVIEWS THE DETAILS
    - The HOD checks:
        • Is your name correct?
        • Is the enrollment number correct?
        • Is the department correct?
        • Are all required documents uploaded?
        • Are certificates valid and readable?
        • Is the thesis title and summary filled?

23. HOD TAKES ACTION
    There are two possible actions:

    A. APPROVE / VERIFY
       - If everything is correct, the HOD clicks “Verify” or “Approve”.
       - Your account is now marked as “Verified”.
       - You will receive a notification and email: “Your account has been
         verified by the HOD.”
       - If you are a Ph.D. scholar, you can now also log in to ScholarTrack.
       - You can now start working on your thesis, milestones, and other
         research activities.

    B. REJECT / SEND BACK
       - If some information is wrong or documents are missing, the HOD clicks
         “Reject”.
       - The HOD must write a remark explaining the reason, for example:
           “Enrollment number is incorrect.”
           “Class 12 certificate is missing.”
           “Thesis title is too vague.”
       - Your profile is marked as “Not Completed” and “Not Verified”.
       - You will receive a notification: “Your profile verification has been
         rejected by HOD. Remarks: …”
       - You must log in again, edit your profile, fix the mistakes, and re-
         submit it.

24. HOD CAN ALSO EDIT YOUR PROFILE
    - If you entered a small mistake, the HOD can directly edit your profile
      and save it.
    - For example, the HOD can correct the spelling of your name, update your
      department, or fix the thesis title.

================================================================================
PART 6 – AFTER HOD VERIFICATION (Ph.D. SCHOLAR WORKFLOW)
================================================================================

For Ph.D. students, after the HOD verifies your profile, you can start your
research journey. The main steps are:

25. CREATE YOUR THESIS RECORD
    - In ScholarSync, go to “Thesis” or “My Thesis”.
    - Click “Create Thesis”.
    - Enter the thesis title, abstract/summary, keywords, specialization, and
      department.
    - Submit it.

26. HOD / ADMIN VERIFIES YOUR ENROLMENT
    - The HOD or Department Admin checks your thesis record.
    - If everything is correct, they click “Verify Enrollment”.

27. SUPERVISOR IS ASSIGNED
    - The HOD or Department Admin assigns a faculty member as your supervisor.
    - You will see the supervisor’s name in your thesis page.

28. COURSEWORK (if applicable)
    - If you have coursework requirements, you must submit the details.
    - Upload proof of completed coursework.
    - Your supervisor and HOD will approve it.

29. RAC REVIEWS (Research Advisory Committee)
    - The system schedules RAC meetings.
    - You or your supervisor may upload RAC reports.
    - The RAC result is recorded.

30. MILESTONES
    - Under “Milestones”, you can submit important documents such as:
        • Synopsis / Provisional Synopsis
        • Pre-submission seminar
        • Final thesis submission
    - Upload the required document and plagiarism report.
    - The supervisor/HOD reviews and approves each milestone.

31. PUBLICATIONS
    - If you have published any research papers, you can submit them under
      “Publications”.
    - Upload the publication document.
    - The HOD or faculty verifies it.

32. DRC MEETINGS (Doctoral Research Committee)
    - DRC meetings are scheduled for reviewing your progress.
    - Results are recorded in the system.

33. FINAL SUBMISSION AND APPROVAL
    - When your thesis is complete, submit the final document.
    - The HOD, supervisor, and external evaluators review it.
    - Viva / seminar is scheduled.
    - After successful evaluation, the degree is awarded.

================================================================================
PART 7 – ATTENDANCE AND LEAVE WORKFLOW (ScholarTrack)
================================================================================

For all students, including Ph.D. students after verification, ScholarTrack
handles attendance and leaves:

34. VIEW YOUR ATTENDANCE
    - Log in to ScholarTrack.
    - Go to “Attendance” or “My Attendance”.
    - You can see your attendance record for each subject.

35. APPLY FOR LEAVE
    - Click “Apply Leave”.
    - Select the date(s), leave type (medical, duty, etc.), and reason.
    - Submit the request.
    - Your faculty / HOD will approve or reject it.

36. ATTENDANCE CORRECTION
    - If your attendance is marked wrong (e.g., you were present but marked
      absent), you can apply for a correction.
    - Go to “Corrections” and submit a request.
    - The faculty / HOD will review and take action.

37. LOW ATTENDANCE WARNING
    - If your attendance is below the required percentage, the system shows a
      warning.
    - You may be listed as a defaulter.
    - Contact your faculty or HOD for guidance.

================================================================================
PART 8 – PASSWORD AND ACCOUNT HELP
================================================================================

38. FORGOT PASSWORD
    - On the login page, click “Forgot Password”.
    - Enter your email address.
    - The system will send a password-reset email.
    - Click the link in the email (valid for 1 hour).
    - Enter a new password and confirm it.
    - You can now log in with the new password.

39. CHANGE PASSWORD / LOGOUT
    - Inside your profile or settings, you can change your password.
    - Always log out when you finish using the system, especially on a shared
      computer.

40. ACCOUNT DISABLED?
    - If the HOD disables your account, you cannot log in.
    - Contact your HOD or Department Admin to know the reason and request
      reactivation.

================================================================================
PART 9 – QUICK SUMMARY OF ROLES
================================================================================

• STUDENT
  - Signs up, verifies email, logs in, completes profile, submits for HOD
    verification, submits thesis/milestones/publications, applies for leave
    and attendance corrections.

• HOD (Head of Department)
  - Verifies or rejects student profiles, manages department users, assigns
    supervisors, approves/rejects milestones, thesis submissions, publications,
    leaves, corrections, and attendance.

• FACULTY / SUPERVISOR
  - Guides the scholar, reviews milestones, approves coursework, schedules
    seminars, marks attendance, approves/rejects leave and correction requests.

• ADMIN / SUPER ADMIN
  - Manages the whole system, creates HOD/faculty accounts, manages departments,
    resets data if needed, configures settings, and has access to all records.
